Game Info

Learn more about the game

Risky Rudi is built upon the three dimensions of risk: hazard, vulnerability and exposure. By explicitly categorising risk reduction measures according to these three dimensions, players develop an intuitive understanding of the multifaceted nature of flood risk and how different types of measures work together to build community resilience.
